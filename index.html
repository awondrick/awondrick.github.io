There's a lot of funky stuff going on out there in both the physical and the meta-physical world. Lot of people spouting out ideas and shit. Trying to drum things up, start a fire.


This here spacial continuum within the World Wide Web is meant to be a place of refuge. A warm and woolen blanket, all woven in intricate patterns, wrapped around your body as the stars shine overhead. The smoke from a fire is billowing softly from smoke stacks, as the city lights twinkle in the distance.


You are standing there, wrapped in your blanket, in the dirt median separating two directions of traffic on the I-85. In front of you is a wide canyon, carved out by ancient floods, and across it a bridge that whispers the triumphs of mankind over its environment.
